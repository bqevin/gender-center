Africa Centre for Gender, Social Research and Impact Assessment

  • Gender Analysis and Training

    We have a roster of consultants with experience in gender analysis in agriculture, general and specialised gender training

  • Evaluation Studies

    We conduct mid term and end of project evaluation studies focused on analysing the social and economic impacts of agriculture research and development programs

  • Integrating Gender in Projects and Programs

    Integrating gender in projects and programs is essential not only for achieving project goals but also for achieving gender equality. Our consultants work with you to ensure gender is integral in your programming

  • Publishing and information sharing

    ACGSRIA publishes the Journal of Gender, Agriculture and Food Security. For more information on the journal, visit www.agrigender.net
    Visit our blog at www.agrigenderjournal.com

  • Supporting rural women's economic empowerment

    Out goal is to implement projects aimed at empowering rural women and youth through agriculture and agribusiness

  • Promoting women and girls in science

    Women are under-represented in science. We aim to implement projects that empower women and girls to excel in science
